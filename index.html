Leviathan — Regime-Aware Housing Risk Research

Leviathan is a macro housing risk research project exploring whether affordability behaves like a regime switch rather than a continuous predictor.

Current extension: Phase V–VII

  • Phase V: defines a fragile affordability regime using DTI threshold behavior
  • Phase VI: tests whether supply expansion amplifies crash probability in fragile regimes
  • Phase VII: backtests a simple risk overlay that exits exposure during fragile + supply stress states
