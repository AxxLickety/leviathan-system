Leviathan — Research Evolution

This page shows how the Leviathan model was gradually simplified through falsification and testing.

1. Initial Hypothesis

Affordability (DTI) + macro regime jointly determine downside risk.

2. Early Signal

DTI appeared predictive in initial synthetic tests.

3. Stress Testing

Percentile DTI, drift sensitivity, and robustness checks weakened the signal.

4. Joint Trigger World

DTI failed to add stable incremental value beyond regime.

5. Model Collapse

DTI removed. Model reduced to regime-only.

6. Multi-Market Validation

Result holds across multiple synthetic markets.

7. Adversarial + Positive Drift

Tradeoff becomes clear: downside protection vs upside cost.

8. Policy Layer

Model becomes a configurable overlay instead of a fixed rule.

Final Insight

Leviathan is what remains after everything else failed.
